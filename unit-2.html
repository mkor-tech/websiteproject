Unit 2, Outcome 1

Create structures and styles for a webpage You are setting up as an independent consultant, which is very exciting, however, you’re going to need a website of your own to highlight your skills, availability and areas of work. It’s time for you to create a website that details you, and including pages/sections and a how you built it page:

  • Home page - Needs immediate visual impact using full view height hero in order to grab attention.
  • Keep the home page simple with large visually pleasing images, used as links for “What we do” and “Some of our work”
  • “What we do” page has a full width hero image, with links detailing services provided. The whole design is structured around a three column grid.
  • “Some of our work” page has a full width hero image and an image grid containing thumbnail links of completed projects. Each project documents the client problem, our solution and the final project outcome.
  • All pages contain a navigation header bar and a footer containing social media links and a link to the contact page, which is also linked in the header navigation.
